Software Guide

Naye users ke liye step-by-step guideline aur categories set karne ka tareeqa.

Step 1: Categories Rename Kaise Karein?

Software har business ke liye hai. "Config" button daba kar aap categories ke naam badal sakte hain. Is se reports aap ki marzi ke mutabiq banengi.

Case A: Aluminum/Glass Shop

  • Rename INC-01 to: Glass Sale
  • Rename EXP-01 to: Labor/Wages
  • Rename INC-05 to: Owner Investment

Case B: Construction/Site

  • Rename INC-08 to: Advance from Client
  • Rename EXP-04 to: Material Purchase
  • Rename OFFICE-BILL to: Site Visit Fuel

Step 2: Borrowing (Loan) Module

Agar aap ne kisi se Qarza (Loan) liya hai to usay "Borrowing Taken" category mein likhein. Dashboard khud hi bata dega ke kitna qarza wapas karna baqi hai.
